About Bhagavad Gita

Srila Prabhupada Ki Jai

Bhagavad Gita is considered to be a Hindu Literature, but it is a literature for all to cultivate their spritual status to go Back to Godhead. It was spoken on the Battlefield of Kurukshetra to Arjuna. It has 700 verses. Krishna spoke 574 verses, Arjuna spoke 84 verses, Sanjaya spoke 41 verses, Dhritarashtra spoke 1 verse. Out of these 700 verses of Bhagavad Gita, there are 108 important verses.
