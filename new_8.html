Saudi Arabia(Introduction)

Saudi Arabia is an Important Arab country. It is a desert. Some of the deserts are: Gobi desert, Atacama desert, etc.

Geography, land and location

Saudi Arabia lies in the Arabian Peninsula, which is the largestin the world. The neighbouring countries are: Jordan, Iraq, Kuwait(north), Bahrain, United Arab Emirates(east), Yemen and Oman(south), Red Sea(west).

Climate

Since the Tropic of Cancer passes through this region, this region is too hot. The nights are pleasant. This is the only contry where the temperature is recorded abov 50 degree C. Asir is the only place which recives rainfall.

Vegetation

Date palm is the only tree that grows easily here.

Wildlife

The most used animal is th camel. It is called the ship of the desert. Other animals include baboons, wolves , ibex, etc.
